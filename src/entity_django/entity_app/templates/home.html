Background and Context

Numerous countries across the world integrate a 'freedom of information' act into their countries laws. A 'freedom of information' act mandates the national government to disclose certain information upon request from countries occupants or following a period of time after the document's creation. Government documents may contain sensitive information such as personal details or trivial information that may harm a countries international relations. Sensitivity reviewers are employed by a countries government to perform sensitivity reviews to identify documents containing sensitive information and either redact individual details or restrict the document itself.

Guide for Usage

Uploading Files

Visiting the 'Upload Files' tab will allow you to upload and process documents for sensitivity review. When uploading files, singular or multiple file selections are supported; a list of selected files can be viewed on the right hand tab of the page. The supported file type for file uploads is .html.gz. When submitting files for upload, please remain on the same tab to ensure the documents are analysed and processed fully.

Documents

The 'Documents' tab displays all uploaded documents currently available for sensitivity review and analysis. The summary of entities and their sensitivities is displayed to give an overview of a document's contents. By using the icon buttons in the right hand column you can view documents in detail, or delete documents from the database. By using the search-bar in the top right of the tab you can filter documents by their filename.

Selecting an individual document will present the document body and additional analysis alongside its contents. Entities within the document will be highlighted with a colour corresponding to their predicted sensitivity. Hovering over an entity will present additional information about the entity to help with understanding the context of its use within the document. Clicking on entities will redirect you to view the entity in more detail. Dynamic plots are featured on the right-hand side of the document presenting the frequency of entities within the document as well as the distribution of predicted sensitivities.

Entities

Selecting the 'Entities' tab allows for an inverted index view, for each entity a short overview of the documents it appears in is available. The predicted sensitivity of each entity is displayed and colour coded to help with reviewing judgements. The right hand column features icons that allow you to view an entity in more detail or delete the entity and its instances from the database.

Selecting an individual entity will allow you to view entity abstracts, its predicted sensitivity as well as the other documents it appears in. The other documents it appears in are available to be viewed in detail or deleted, if the number of documents exceeds the window size a scroll bar will be featured to assist in navigation.

Analytics

The 'Analytics' page offers extra insight into the overall trends of the documents uploaded, with the main feature of the analytics page being Latent Dirichlet Topic modelling. LDA Topic Modelling clusters documents into a given number of topics (in this instance, eight topics), with each topic made up of related entities with weights corresponding to the contribution they've made to make up the topic. The donut charts on display present related entities with corresponding related documents appearing on the right of tab.
